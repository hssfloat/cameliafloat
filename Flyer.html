About Our Float
The Hindu Americans of San Gabriel Valley invite you to witness our breathtaking Holi – Festival of Colors float as it rolls through the streets in a burst of powder, petals, and pride.

Our float celebrates Holi's ancient spirit — the arrival of spring, the victory of good over evil, and the joyful bond that unites our community. Adorned with marigolds, rangoli patterns, and flowing clouds of vibrant color, the float honors the rich traditions of South Asian heritage while embracing our home here in the San Gabriel Valley.

Come join thousands of spectators and let the colors of Holi fill your heart!
